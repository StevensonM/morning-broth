tech therapy
Pierre Rambeau is here to stay

Morning Broth sat down with famous Silicon Valley tech therapist Pierre Rambeau to talk mental health.

How can investors cope with the inevitable dip as we go into a recession?

“You know, I've thought a lot about this. I think it helps to hunt the good in life. When I was twelve, I stood in a field watching clouds form. They hung and boiled. Lightning struck like flint through balls of dark cotton. It rained and I stood there soaking. Through the flashes and tall grass, I saw a doe leading her fawn towards the tree line. As an adult, I look back on this moment often and think about how life gives us little nuggets to hold onto forever and how we come to depend on them.”

Notable valley investors Chris Sacca, Reid Hoffman, and Ben Horowitz have all very publicly sang your praises. What are some commonalities they all share?

"I've been very luck to work with those people. Oddly enough, all of those people subscribe to Morning Broth."
